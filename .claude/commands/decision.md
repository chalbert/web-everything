Invoke the `next-backlog-item` skill in **decision mode** (the same as `/next decision`): invert the
default build-first bias and surface the single highest-leverage open design decision to *make* now,
rather than a dev-ready build. Present the ranked decision shortlist. When I pick one — I say `go`, or I
name an item below (e.g. `100` or `100-requirement-as-code`) — that is the trigger to **claim it and
present it for discussion**, NOT to ratify it. Claiming is its own turn (claim, emit the rename slug,
STOP). Then present the decision's substance for back-and-forth, red-team the default, and ratify +
close it out **only once I explicitly say so after we've discussed**. Naming an item skips the
*selection* step — never the claim → discuss → ratify arc; do not collapse those into one turn.
