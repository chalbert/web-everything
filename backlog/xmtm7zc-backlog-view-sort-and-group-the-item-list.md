# Backlog-view: sort and group the item list

Add sort and grouping controls to the list — sort by item number, size, or status (ascending or descending; the loader's default is newest-number-first), and optionally group rows into sections by status or by epic / parent. Gives the reader a way to organise a large backlog around the axis they care about, entirely client-side over the loaded rows.

**Acceptance:** at least number / size / status sorts in both directions; a group-by-status view with per-section counts; the active sort / group is reflected in the header and resets cleanly.
