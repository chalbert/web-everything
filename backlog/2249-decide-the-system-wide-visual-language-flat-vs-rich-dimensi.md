# Decide the system-wide visual language — flat-minimal vs rich-dimensional

The single most upstream branding decision: does the constellation's visual language stay
**flat-minimal** (current: solid white glyphs on gradient squircles) or move to **rich-dimensional**
(light, shadow, depth)? This changes *every* mark AND the website, so it gates the rest of #2256.

Probe rendered (`plateau:branding-proposals/loop/plateau-rich-a.svg` = light+shadow, promising;
`plateau-rich-b.svg` = full 3D, heavier; vs the current flat mesa) — see the Plateau probe round on
`plateau:branding.html#journeys`. Prepare: apply flat vs rich-a to all three marks side by side at
220/64/16 (via the `brand-mark-loop` skill) and rule one. Codifies into #2209's construction-system
section on ratify.
