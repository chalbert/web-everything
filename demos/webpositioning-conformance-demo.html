Web Positioning Conformance Demo

The runnable proof of the PositioningStrategy contract (#1048): a surface declares intent — a Placement (bottom-start, top-end, …) plus collision flags (flip / shift / resize) — and a swappable strategy realizes it and returns a reversible PositioningTeardown.

The contract is type-only (positioning/contract.ts); the native CSS-anchor strategy, the JS fallback, and the customPositioning swap registry are impl and live in Frontier UI. So this demo supplies its own in-demo JS placement strategy — honest for a browser demo — to prove the contract is realizable.
