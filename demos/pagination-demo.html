Pagination Playground

The runnable home for the Pagination block — the blocks-layer realization of the Collection Operations intent's page stage. Each card pairs a page-state with a contract (pageMode × advance, urlSync, rangeLabel), renders the controls through the reference renderer, and runs the same a11y/SEO audit the CI conformance suite asserts.

A green badge means the rendered controls satisfy the verified contract: a <nav aria-label="pagination"> landmark with aria-current="page" on the active page, self-canonical ?page=n links as the SEO default, the result-range label only over a known total, and infinite scroll rendered as a scroll sentinel (append + auto) rather than a peer mode. The renderer here is a deterministic reference; concrete strategies live in Frontier UI.
