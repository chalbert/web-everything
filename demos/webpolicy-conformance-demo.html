Web Policy Conformance Demo

The runnable proof of the webpolicy DMN contract+engine and proof-of-compliance (#1028, slice C). A DMN-style decision table is evaluated through the real PolicyDecisionPoint under each hit policy (FIRST / UNIQUE / PRIORITY / default), then every decision is appended to a hash-linked ProofChain that verifies — and a tampered chain is caught.

The app knows only the PolicyRuleSet meta-schema (#406); the rule-evaluator is the swappable seam (#093). The demo hash is a deterministic non-crypto stub — a real venue injects SHA-256; the chaining/tamper logic under test is identical.
