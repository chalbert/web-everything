Declarative <script type="injector"> (#278)

A <script type="injector"> binds its parent subtree to a domain (a Protocol identity, per #002) and the impl provided for it — no build step, no resolver. Descendants inherit the domain implicitly; an element with injector="id" binds to a named injector regardless of position.
