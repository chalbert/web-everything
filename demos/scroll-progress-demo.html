Scroll Progress — the 0..1 driver

The Scroll Progress intent produces a continuous 0..1 value from scroll position. This page drives two effects from it, native-first: a reading-progress bar (reference: scroll) and a parallax reveal (reference: view). Where the browser supports CSS Scroll-driven Animations the work is compositor-driven with zero JS; otherwise the @supports fallback drives the same progress from a scroll handler / IntersectionObserver. Under prefers-reduced-motion both resolve to a static end-state.
