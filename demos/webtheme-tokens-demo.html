Web Theme — Tokens, Schemes & High Contrast

The DTCG token document compiles to native CSS at runtime: a :root custom-property block (compileToCss), plus a derived scheme + accent ramp under color-scheme and a @media (prefers-contrast: more) high-contrast pair (compileSchemeCss).
