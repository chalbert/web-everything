pickup once each lands (one wave only — don't pre-scaffold the whole tail).

**DAG:** all three independent (no inter-slice `blockedBy`); each leaves a valid demoable state (decisions
mutate no code). Conversion tasks are downstream of their decision, filed on pickup.
