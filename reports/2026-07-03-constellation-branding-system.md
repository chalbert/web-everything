## 4. Methodology survey (Fork 6 grounding — durable for #2207)

Three practitioner methods for judging brand work:

- **Attribute-driven rubric** — the design-system voice-and-tone / brand-expression pattern: 3–4
  attributes + one target feeling per brand; candidates scored against the owning set; every verdict
  cites the attribute it serves/violates. Ancestry: Aaker's brand-personality dimensions.
  *Checklist-scoreable → the only shape an AI judge can be regression-tested against.*
- **Archetype framework** — Jungian archetypes (WE ≈ Sage, FUI ≈ Explorer, Plateau ≈ Ruler); rich
  narrative, fuzzy scoring, persuasion-marketing framing overshoots dev-facing repos.
- **Uncodified taste** — the status quo; produced exactly the drift tier in §1–2 and leaves an AI
  reviewer nothing to ground in.

**Proposed rubric (Fork 6 default):** WE *universal · principled · inevitable* / calm trust; FUI
*pioneering · pragmatic · kinetic* / capable momentum; Plateau *elevated · steady · commanding* /
assured overview. **Landscape narrative:** bedrock → horizon → plateau — WE is the ground, FUI the
horizon where new ground is claimed, Plateau the high ground you operate from.

Mark/icon prior art applied per fork: size-tuned favicon simplification (GitHub invertocat-only
favicon, Firefox 2019 simplified marks) → Fork 2; symbol-over-letterform small-size quality → Fork 3;
Lucide/Feather mechanical stroke+grid enforcement and Material Symbols keylines → the `check:branding`
gate; DTCG token layer as color source of truth (#1283) → Fork 4.
