This Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") documents the rights granted by

to Apache-2.0), and the productized layer is proprietary (see

gradient to remain honest and legally sound, the Steward must hold the right to
**license and relicense** every contribution across those tiers. A
[Developer Certificate of Origin](https://developercertificate.org/) (DCO) alone is
**not sufficient** here: it certifies a contributor's right to submit, but grants no
relicensing rights. This Agreement does.

## How to sign

Contributions are gated by the **CLA Assistant** bot (see
[`.github/workflows/cla.yml`](.github/workflows/cla.yml)). When You open Your first
pull request to any Project, the bot will comment with a link to sign. Signing is a
one-time action recorded against Your GitHub identity and applies to all future
Contributions to the Projects.
